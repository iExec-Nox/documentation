# System Status

At Nox Protocol, we are committed to providing a reliable infrastructure and maintaining full transparency with our users. 

You can monitor the real-time status, incident reports, and the 90-day historical uptime of all our core services on our dedicated status page:

👉 **[status.noxprotocol.io](https://status.noxprotocol.io/)**

## Monitored Components

When you visit the status page, you can check the operational status of our key infrastructure components:

* **[Nox Handle Gateway](/nox-protocol/protocol/handle-gateway)**
* **[Nox KMS](/nox-protocol/protocol/kms)**
* **[Nox Runner](/nox-protocol/protocol/runner)**
* **[Nox Ingestor](/nox-protocol/protocol/ingestor)**

## How to use this page?

* **Check for Outages:** If you are experiencing unexpected timeouts, errors, or connectivity issues, please check the status page first to see if there is an active incident affecting the specific component you are using.
* **Subscribe to Updates:** We highly recommend clicking the **"Get updates"** button in the top right corner of the status page. You can subscribe via email or RSS to receive instant notifications whenever an incident is created, updated, or resolved.
